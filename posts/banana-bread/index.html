Banana Bread

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.

In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
In a separate large bowl, beat brown sugar and butter with an electric mixer until smooth. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended.
Stir banana mixture into flour mixture until just combined. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.

Bake about 60 minutes until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
